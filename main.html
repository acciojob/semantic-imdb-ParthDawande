Movie Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who enters the subconscious of others to steal or implant ideas. When offered a chance to clear his criminal record, Cobb embarks on a dangerous mission that blurs the line between dreams and reality.
